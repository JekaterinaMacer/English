Question no. 1: Can you tell me something about yourself?

This is often the very first question. It helps the HR managers to get a basic idea of your communication skills, motivation, and interests. It is also an ice breaker, and a good answer will help you to feel more relaxed.

The interviewers do not ask about your education, experience, personal life, or anything else in particular—you can choose the way to introduce yourself. However, your choice reflects what matters to you. In a job interview, you should talk about your education, working experience, career goals, skills and abilities. You should talk about things that are relevant for the employer.

On the other hand, you can mention one or two hobbies, or tell them something from your personal life. This shows that you have a life outside of work.


Start with the general information

  • My name (first name) is Pavel.  I am Pavel Petrov.
  • My surname (last name) is Petrov.
  • I am 33 years old.
  • I was born in Moscow and I have lived all my life here.
  • I live in St. Graziasburg.
  • I’m married (not married).
  • I’m widowed.
  • I’m single.
  • I’m divorced.
  • I have two (no) children.


Continue with your education

  • I graduated from the Moscow State Institute of International Relations in 2003.
  • I graduated from the university with a specialty in computer science. 
  • I received my master’s degree in Economics in 2001.
  • I am currently studying at the Southern Federal University.
  • I received an international certificate in accounting in 2005.
  • I studied at college in the USA. 
  • While I studied at university, I worked as an intern. After I graduated, I worked in the same place full-time.


Then tell about your work experience

  • I worked as an account for 5 years.
  • My position was a credit control manager and I was responsible for market analysis.
  • I worked in the main office.
  • I am currently employed in the bank as a back office manager.
  • I was dealing with internal and external trade.
  • The key aspect of my job was providing technical data.


Obligatory tell about your personality

  • I am hard-working and responsible person.
  • I have excellent communication skills.
  • I’m very punctual. I am always on time for work.
  • I’m very active and organized.
  • I always do my best to achieve professional goals. 
  • I can deal with difficult situations. 
  • I am an experienced specialist with extensive knowledge.
  • My major strength is the ability to work under pressure.
  • I specialize in Fixed Assets


Also you can tell about your hobby, but quite short

  • My hobby is playing chess.
  • I am fond of doing sports.
  • I am interested in music.


Repeating different sentences 

I received my master’s degree in Economics in 2001.

I was dealing with internal and external trade.

I am an experienced specialist with extensive knowledge.

I always do my best to achieve professional goals.

 (Repeat it with the different emotions)

Example:

I am Ekaterina, 38 years old, I’m married and I have one child. 

I received the Master degree in Economy in 2007 and I have been working as an accountant for 11 years.

I am an experienced specialist with extensive knowledge. My major strength is the ability to work under pressure. Also I can deal with difficult situations. I’m very punctual. I am always on time for work.

I am looking for the new job, ideally in a big company.

In my free time I like swimming and meeting with friends. I’m also fond of reading detective books.
